The Problem

Parking your van in open air can be fun but it also can be dangerous or unsafe.

The Solution

Campersurfing helps Camper fans who are willing to find new places with people that can host them in their private parking space.

Advantage

Why not host a camper in you private parking spot and meet new people?

The Idea

Who travels by camper has already experienced the sense of freedom and joy that one could feel only in free camping.

Any camper traveller knows pretty well that it is not that easy to find out any places in campings, especially during high season period. It is possible to stay in equipped areas, but it might be dangerous to get far from the van or even to leave it unattended for a while.

To discover new paths, to visit barely known places or far from the most common touristic seats is amazing, but sometimes it might be a tough task to combine "free destination" and "free stops" with a"safe parking".

This is the reason why we are developing "Campersurfing", a platform for passionate free camper drivers who want to meet people available to host them in their own parking and to help them in discovering the treasures of their secret places. FAQ

Is CamperSurfing free of charge?

Yes, the registration is free, while a small compensation will be given to the host of the parking service offered by CamperSurfing.

In our opinion, a small bill is the right compensation for those who host a camper. The fees will be agreed by the guest and the host, in accordance to the possible extra services the host could offer, such as water tank filling, electrical wires and/or connections, access to laundry, and so on.

For sure, a host service will never be as professional as a camping one, but there is plenty of small things that could make the staying much more confortable both for the guest and for the host.

Is CamperSurfing legal?

Campersurfing simply put through camper drivers and common people (available to the host them). Some countries require a declaration of the fee paid, although of small amounts, for taxes purpouses, but some other countries do not require any. If you have any doubts, we kindly suggest you to get in touch with your tax office or with your accountant to get further info.

Is Campersurfing alternative to the camping?

No, we do not want to create a new connection web as a surrogate to the traditional camps: our aim consists in offering the travellers the chance to visit and stay places out of the common "turists ways".

Campersurfing is not alternative to the traditional camps, but a further opportunity to visit new places and to get accommodated right where that was not even conceivable. .

Is the park safe in a CamperSurfing park?

Campersurfing is not created as an alternative to a common camping, where your staying is granted by a professional service. On the other hand, several travellers do not feel safe when parking in isolated places or uncommon areas. Our idea is that of staying in a private place is much safer, both for people and for personal belongings.

Parking at a host of Campersurfing will allow you to leave your camper there and spend your time in hiking, travelling and visiting any interesting places in the neighbourhood. Campersurfing, thanks to feedback and evaluation system, will allow to evaluate the quality of the staying and of the services available at the host's place. In any case, Campersurfing will not be responsible for any claim or other legal issues.
